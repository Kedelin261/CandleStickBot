── CLASSIFICATION ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

VERDICT: SYNTHETIC_SUSPECT

Evidence:
  1. 34.8% exact doji rate (expected < 1% on real feed) — STRONG indicator
  2. 100% zero volume — definitive indicator of synthetic/reconstructed data
  3. Perfectly uniform DOW distribution — improbable on real data
  4. 2014-01-01 market holiday bar included — real feeds exclude this
  5. Spot checks show 4–10 pip deviations from known closes (years 2016–2025)
  6. No inter-week gaps even for major holidays — real feeds have gaps

The price levels are historically plausible (1.37 in 2014, 0.97 in Sept 2022,
1.10 in 2024) suggesting the data may be derived from real price history via
resampling, model-generation, or synthetic reconstruction — but it cannot be
classified as a real broker or exchange feed.
